When viewing my page on different browsers, I noticed the DOB part of my form
differed depending what browser I used. On Chrome and Safari, the DOB could be
filled out by using the arrows or selecting the date on a calendar. However, on
Edge, the DOB could be filled out with a scroll-through pop up; with separate
selections for month, day, and year.

Originally, when you click on the text next to each radio element in the form,
it did not put the corresponding field into focus. This ends up affecting the
user experience since the tiny radio button can be difficult to precisely click.
Thus, I changed the form code so that the corresponding text for each radio
button could be clicked, which would put the field into focus.
